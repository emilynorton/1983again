It's 1983 again! A nostalgic animation project.

About this project

In 1983, I was a senior in high school and had my first computer science class. We used Apple IIes and wrote in BASIC. Good times. I was one of the few people who really "got it." As I recall, many in the class were irritated with me. My teacher loved me. Note: I did end up studing computer science in college, but it was not my major because my GPA was too low. Sigh. I did take a computer science class most semester of college.
